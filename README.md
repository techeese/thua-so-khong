# Thừa Số Không — The Zero Factor

**A feel-first prototype** of `../steve_vietnam_plan_fable/THESIS.md` — the multiplication thesis as a game you play
at human scale. One small xóm, six named people, sixteen seasons.

- **The core verb is DIAGNOSIS:** tap a person, hear their story, find which factor sits at zero
  (TÀI × GAN × BẠN — the card literally shows the multiplication; one zero kills the product).
- **Your hands are limited:** 3 công/season on people-locks (teach · failure-night · connect), a hụi pool,
  and your own product to ship (an existence-proof that gives everyone +GAN).
- **The untouchable locks are the landscape:** *bầu trời là luật* (the sky = the law; heavy sky can stamp a
  workshop ĐÌNH CHỈ), *dòng sông là vốn* (the river = capital; low river keeps every workshop a street stall).
  They drift on their own — you cannot click them.

## Process notes (deliberate inversions vs. Vườn Ươm)
- **FEEL → MODEL**, not MODEL → SWEEP → UI: this file exists to answer one question — *does finding a zero feel
  like play?* — before any balance discipline is spent on it.
- **Human gate only.** No critique panels, no sweep. The gate is the owner playing ~5 minutes and answering:
  1. Does diagnosing a person's zero feel like *play* (not homework)?
  2. Do you care about anyone (Bé Ngân? Chú Ba?) by season 8?
  3. Did "mười nhân với không vẫn bằng không" land without reading THESIS?
- Throwaway by intent: if the answers are yes, THEN it earns engine/sweep/gates + the Đông Hồ art pass.

Run: open `index.html`. Bilingual (VI/EN toggle). Desktop + mobile.
